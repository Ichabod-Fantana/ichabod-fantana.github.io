What is League of Legends?
Home Step 1 Step 2 Step 3 Step 4

League of Legends, or Lol for short, is a MOBA (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena) game where two teams of 5 compete to destroy the other team's base. There are over one hundred different characters, or champions to play, so there's one to fit everyone's playstyle. LoL is currently the most popular multiplayer game in the entire world, with about 27 million people playing every single day.

LoL also falls into the classification of an e-sport. There are different competitions all over the world. Some of the top regions being Korea, China, Europe, and North America. The prize for winning? Lots, of money. At Worlds this year, the biggest of all the competitions, the prize pool for the team that won was $5,000,000. Forigners can even come into the US to compete with an Athlete's Visa because the Supreme Court ruled that LoL was a sport.

League of Legends was released in 2009 and has undergone many changes since its inception. The game is updated in a steady stream of patches. These patches can slightyl, or sometimes very majorly affect the game. A patch might give a champion slightly more, or slightly less damage, or a patch could change an entire champion and how they work, so don't expect everything to be a constant. Everything in the game is always subject to change.
