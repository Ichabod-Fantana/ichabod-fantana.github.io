In LoL, there are five roles that you can choose from. They are ADC (Attack Damage Carry), Support, Middle, Top, and Jungler. Each of these roles have a different place in the team.
The ADC is going to be a ranged champion with high damage output, and low health. The ADC will usually have the most damage on the team and will be invaluably for taking objectives. The ADC will always be in the bottom lane.
The Support is a champion that helps the ADC. This can be acomplished different ways. Some supports can stun enemy champions, some can heal allies, and some have lots of health and can sponge up damage for their ADC/team. The Support is so important becase they can give ADCs the healing, or crowd control (A Stun or Root) that they need. The support will also be in the bottom lane.
The Mid Laner is usually going to be a Utility, or Damage Mage. A Utility Mage can do things like stun/root enemy champions, or teleport/move quickly. The Mid Laner is such a major part of the team because since they are in the middle of the map, then can roam to the other two lanes to help out. The Mid Laner will be in the Middle Lane.
The Top Laner is usually going to be the team's tank. The top laner will usually have some sort of utility, like a stun, or a slow, or a blind (An Ability That Prevetns The Enemy From Auto Attacking). The Top Laner will always be in the Top Lane.
