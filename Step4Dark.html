Towers and The Nexus

So, by now, you should've have one or two roles to chose from when you play, and a small champion pool to chose from. Now its time to learn how to win League of Legends. You should know that to win you need to push into the enemy base by taking their towers and eventually destroying their Nexus, while defending yours. But you can't blindly push a lane and get into their base. There are certian things you must do. The most important thig is taking objectives. The best ones to take being towers. But there are three other objectives you can take. They are the assorted drakes, Rift Herald, and Baron. Each one will give a buff to an individual champion of an entire team. The most common of these to take are the drakes.

Drakes & Elder Dragon

There are four drakes, and one dragon. The drakes are Cloud, Mountian, Infernal, and Ocean. The dragon is the Elder dragon. The Cloud Drake gives out of combat movement speed, the Mountian Drake gives extra damage to towers and Epic Monsters (Including other drakes/dragons, Rift Herald, and Baron). Infernal will give you a bonus percentage of Attack Damage or Ability Power. Ocean Drake will give extra health and mana regeneration. Finially, the Elder will make the buffs you have from slaying Elemental Drakes stronger.

Rift Herald & Baron

The two final objectives are Baron and Rift Herald. The Rift Herald will spawn in the first 20 minutes and afterwards be replaced by Baron. Rift Herald will give a buff so a single champion after it is slain. It can redice the famage you take when you're not fighiting alongside an ally. It can also deal magic damage to enemy units. The Baron's buff is argurably the strongest in the game. When you are near your own minions, they will become buffed. They will get much stronger and do much more damage. This is very useful when pushing into the enemy's base as your minions will be harder to kill, allowing you to stay longer and do more damage to the enemy's turrets.
