Step 2: Account Creation/Downloading

To get started playing LoL, you're going to need an account. To do this, head over the the League of Legends website and create one. Once you've created your account, you should be ale to click the, play game button. This will start a download for the Client. The Client is how you'll play LoL. It's a program on your computer that you'll start up like any other, and you can find a game within that. The first time you start the client after downloading, it will need to set up the game. This can take several hours, so you should find something else to do during this time.

After The Client is finished downloading everything, you should be able to click the big orange Launch button. This will bring you to the sign in menu. All you have to is enter your Summoner Name and your password that you hopefully set up earlier. If everything goes smoothly, you should be logged in and see the Home Page. You can then start the tutorials the game gives you.

To start playing you can click the large orange Play button. This will bring you to a menu where you can chose game type. If you are a beginner, you should chose to fight AI. This will put you in a team with real people on your side, and the other team is made up of robots. Pick a champion, and follow the tutorials and tips the game gives you.
